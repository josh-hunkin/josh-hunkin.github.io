What's the difference between margin, border & padding?

Border:

The border allows you to change the size/color of the contents border, whether you want to emphasize something with style or simply change the border color.



Padding:

Padding is the space between the border and whatever it is surrounding your content, this can be applied from top, bottom, left and right to intentionally separate or move your content.



Margin:

Margin is the space outside the edge of the border. When margin is made greater/lesser, the empty space outside of the content grows or lessens. I say empty because the space will be transparent.
