Neuroplasticity & Growth Mindset

Neuroplasticity

From what I currently understand, neuroplasticity is the term to describe the fact that your brain is malleable, just like plastic, hence 'plasticity'. This means that it is possible to learn anything, and I mean anything. It sounds amazing right!? Of course this only comes with consistent practice and repitition to form and strengthen the pathways involved in storing this information.



Growth Mindset

From what I understand at this point in time, the growth mindset severely affects the way we live. What I mean by this is our thoughts, our intentions, and our mindset shape us. To have a growth mindset, you have to face challenges with a light heart and disregard the possibility failure. Failure is an essential to progression. To have a growth mindset, you always keep in mind that intelligence is something you work on and develop over time. It doesn't come free, but its totally possible with the practice to develop.
