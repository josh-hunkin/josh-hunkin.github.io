Emotional intelligence



My understanding of emotional intelligence

All aspects of social interaction play a part to someones overall emotional intelligence, whether it's recognising others emotions, or ability to give your undivided attention to someone. But from what I understand, the key aspect to emotional intelligence is to literally be intelligent with your own and others emotions. What I mean by this is know how you imapct others with your emotions, know when to show empathy, know when to care for your own emotions, just in general know to be aware of your surroundings.
